How It Works
1
Collar reads sensors every 30 seconds
Temperature, heart rate, and accelerometer data captured continuously
2
LoRa radio sends data every 10-15 minutes
Low-power radio transmits to farm gateway up to 5km away
3
Farm gateway collects all collar data
One gateway per farm handles the entire herd and pushes to cloud via WiFi or cellular
4
Cloud cleans and stores time-series data
Data is validated, timestamped, and stored per animal
5
AI model checks each cow against her baseline
Three-layer system compares readings to each animal's personal norm
6
Push notification sent to your phone
Alert delivered within minutes of anomaly detection
AI Layers
1
Rule-Based Alerts Day 1
Works immediately, no data needed. Fixed thresholds trigger the moment a reading crosses a limit.
2
LSTM Autoencoder 2-3 weeks
Learns each cow personally. Flags deviations from her own norm, not species averages. Catches subtle patterns rules miss.
3
Chronos Fine-Tuned 6 months
Predicts events 24-48h before they are visible. Fine-tuned Amazon time-series model trained on cattle data.
