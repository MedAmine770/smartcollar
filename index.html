What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Three sensors, one gateway, and three layers of AI running continuously on every collar in your herd.

Temperature — every 30s
Core body temperature captured continuously. Rule-based alerts fire the moment a reading exceeds 39.8°C. No setup needed, works from day one.
Activity and rumination
Accelerometer tracks movement patterns and detects rumination. A drop in chewing time is often the first clinical sign of illness — days before fever appears.
Heart rate (PPG)
Optical pulse sensor provides continuous heart rate readings. Combined with temperature deviation, it significantly reduces false alarms.
Personal AI baseline
After 2-3 weeks, the LSTM model learns each cow's individual norm. Alerts compare against her personal baseline — not a population average.
24-48h early prediction
Chronos, a time-series AI fine-tuned on cattle data, predicts illness, estrus, and calving before they are clinically visible.
One gateway per farm
A single LoRa gateway covers your full herd up to 5km. No per-collar SIM cards. This is what keeps the collar affordable at $35-55.
End to end

From collar to your phone
in under 15 minutes.

1
Collar reads sensors
Temperature, heart rate, and movement captured every 30 seconds and processed on-device by a lightweight edge AI chip.
2
LoRa transmits to gateway
Every 10-15 minutes, the collar sends a data packet to the farm gateway — up to 5km, no line of sight required.
3
Cloud runs the AI
Three layers of AI check each reading against that cow's personal baseline in real time.
4
You get an alert
Push notification with the exact reading, AI confidence, likely causes, and a step-by-step action plan.
5
Confirm or dismiss
Your feedback is logged as labeled training data. The model sharpens for your specific herd over time.
6
Add collars in 60 seconds
Scan the QR code on the collar label. The collar is named and monitoring immediately.
