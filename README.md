## Simulator

**[► Open the Live Simulator](https://medamine770.github.io/smartcollar/graze.html)**

The `graze.html` simulator lets you:

- Watch 120 cattle move through day/night cycles (grazing → feeding → resting)
- Adjust GPS signal, camera coverage, and RFID density — see how tracking quality affects early disease detection
- Trigger an outbreak and watch the AI flag affected animals in real time
- View disease detail pages with affected animal lists, causes, and vet action plans
- Explore 4 disease profiles: BRD, Foot-and-Mouth, Mastitis, Heat Stress

---

Built as a single-file HTML prototype. No backend, no dependencies except Chart.js CDN.
